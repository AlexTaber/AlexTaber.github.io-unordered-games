Description

Vroomies is a racing game tailor made for touch controls! Your car moves forward automatically. Simply swipe in the correct direction driving over circles, and tap when driving over squares. The better you time your taps, the faster you'll go! Race your Vroomie to victory against your adorably fierce competition!

Features

Inventive Gameplay

Vroomies provides a fresh take on the racing genre, blending elements of rhythm games to create an irresistible loop. Rather than chasing high scores and rankings, players earn lower times and finishing positions in races!

Championship Mode

Put your racing mettle to the test in 4 Championships! Race to the top of the standings, unlocking Upgrades and Challenges for a fresh experience every time you play. It'll take a good strategy and quick thumbs to make it to the top - do you have what it takes?

Global Leaderboards

See where you stack up against the fastest Vroomies in the world!

Play Your Way

Experiment with 12 unique Upgrades and 3 Vroomie Types to create the perfect build for each race!
